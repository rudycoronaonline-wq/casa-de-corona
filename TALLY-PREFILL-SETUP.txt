Tally form pre-fill — one-time setup
====================================
When guests submit the booking form on your site, they are sent to your Tally form with their answers pre-filled so they don't have to type everything again. They only need to click Submit on Tally.

For the pre-fill to work, add these as HIDDEN fields in your Tally form (form editor → type /hidden):

  first_name
  last_name
  email
  phone
  check_in
  check_out
  guests
  how_heard
  special_requests

Then, for each visible field that should show the guest’s answer, set its “Default answer” to use the matching hidden field. Tally help: https://tally.so/help/pre-populate-form-fields
