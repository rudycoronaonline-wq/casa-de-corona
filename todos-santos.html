What to do Destination

Todos Santos

A pueblo mágico on the Pacific side. Art, history, and farm-to-table dining in a relaxed, walkable town.

Art Galleries

Todos Santos has a strong arts scene. Stroll the historic center and pop into galleries and workshops. You'll find painting, sculpture, and crafts from local and expat artists.

Hotel California

The famous Hotel California (yes, that one) is a real place — now a hotel, restaurant, and bar. Even if you don't stay, it's worth a look and a drink. The town's legend and the Eagles' song make it a must-see for many visitors.

Farm-to-Table & Dining

Fresh, local ingredients are a big draw. Many restaurants source from nearby farms and the sea. Great for a long lunch or dinner after a morning at Cerritos or in town.
