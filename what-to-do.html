Your Baja California Base Camp

Everything Within Reach

Casa de Corona sits in El Tezal, perched above Cabo San Lucas with panoramic views of the Sea of Cortez and the iconic Arch — just 10 minutes from the marina, world-class beaches, and championship golf. It is the perfect base for exploring one of the most diverse travel destinations in Mexico.

The tip of the Baja California peninsula packs an extraordinary range of experiences into a compact geography. On any given day you can kayak sea caves at sunrise, eat fresh ceviche at a beachside palapa, surf a point break, explore a colonial art town, and watch the sunset paint the desert hills gold from your own private terrace.
