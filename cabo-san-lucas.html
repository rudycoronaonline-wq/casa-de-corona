About Cabo San Lucas

Cabo San Lucas sits at the southern tip of the Baja California peninsula, where the Sea of Cortez meets the Pacific Ocean. The famous natural arch at Land's End — El Arco — is Cabo's postcard, and the marina is the launch point for sport fishing, water taxis, sunset cruises, and glass-bottom boats. Medano Beach offers the main swimmable shoreline with calm water, palapa restaurants, and water sports.

The marina boardwalk is lined with restaurants, bars, and activity operators, making it an all-day destination that comes alive at sunset. Cabo is one of the world's top sportfishing destinations — marlin, dorado, tuna, and more. Half- and full-day charters leave from the marina; book in advance in peak season.

Beyond the water, downtown Cabo has a more local feel for dining and nightlife, while Medano and the corridor offer beach clubs and a resort atmosphere.

Combine Cabo San Lucas with San José del Cabo 25 minutes east — art district, historic mission, and farm-to-table dining. A perfect contrast to the marina energy.

Top Things To Do

El Arco & Land's End

The famous natural arch at Land's End is Cabo's postcard. Take a water taxi or boat tour from the marina to see the arch up close, and often sea lions on the rocks. Round trip about $15 from the marina dock. Best in morning light.

Medano Beach

The main swimmable beach in Cabo, with calm water, palapa restaurants, and water sports. Great for families and a full day in the sun.

Sport Fishing

Cabo is one of the world's top sportfishing destinations. Marlin, dorado, tuna, and more. Half- and full-day charters leave from the marina — book in advance in peak season.

Marina & Nightlife

Stroll the marina for dinner and people-watching. For nightlife, Medano and the corridor have bars and clubs; downtown Cabo has a more local feel.

El Arco Water Taxi

~$15 round trip from marina. Sea lions, Playa del Amor. Best light: early morning.

Sportfishing Charter

Shared panga from ~$120/person. Private yacht $800–$2,000/day. Book ahead in peak season.

Sunset Catamaran

~$65–95/person with open bar. Departs marina ~5pm. El Arco in golden hour.

Medano Beach Day

Swimmable water, palapas, water sports. Families and full-day sun.

Glass-Bottom Boat

$15–20/person. El Arco, sea lions, marine life. No reservation needed.

Marina Dining

From tacos to high-end seafood. El Squid Roe for nightlife; Mocambo for fresh fish.
