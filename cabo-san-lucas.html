What to do Destination

Cabo San Lucas

Where the Sea of Cortez meets the Pacific. Iconic rock arches, beaches, sport fishing, and a buzzing marina.

El Arco & Land's End

The famous natural arch at Land's End is Cabo's postcard. Take a water taxi or boat tour from the marina to see the arch up close, and often sea lions on the rocks. Best in morning light.

Medano Beach

The main swimmable beach in Cabo, with calm water, palapa restaurants, and water sports. Great for families and a full day in the sun.

Sport Fishing

Cabo is one of the world's top sportfishing destinations. Marlin, dorado, tuna, and more. Half- and full-day charters leave from the marina — book in advance in peak season.

Marina & Nightlife

Stroll the marina for dinner and people-watching. For nightlife, Medano and the corridor have bars and clubs; downtown Cabo has a more local feel.
