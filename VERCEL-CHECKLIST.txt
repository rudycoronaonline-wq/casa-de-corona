WHY "casa-de-corona" DOESN'T SHOW IN VERCEL
==========================================
The project is only on your computer. It will NOT appear in your Vercel 
dashboard until you deploy it once. Nobody can do that for you—you must 
log in and run the deploy from your machine.

DO THESE 3 THINGS (one time)
=============================

1. Double-click:  deploy-to-vercel.bat
   (It's in this same folder: casa-de-corona)

2. When your browser opens → sign in to Vercel (GitHub or email).
   Then go back to the black window.

3. When it asks "Set up and deploy?" type  Y  and press Enter.
   Keep pressing Enter for any other questions.

When it finishes you'll see a link like:
   https://casa-de-corona-xxxx.vercel.app

4. Open:  https://vercel.com/dashboard
   Your project will now be in the list (might say "casa-de-corona" or 
   the name you chose).
