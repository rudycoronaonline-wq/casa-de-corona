What to do Destination

Cerritos Beach

Pacific waves, surf lessons, and sunset vibes. A relaxed beach day near Todos Santos.

Surf Lessons

Cerritos is one of the best spots in the area to learn to surf. Gentle rollers and several schools right on the beach. Book a lesson for the morning when the wind is lighter; boards and wetsuits are available to rent.

Pacific Sunset

Face west for a classic Pacific sunset over the water. Bring a chair or post up at one of the beachfront restaurants for drinks and dinner as the sun goes down.

Beach Lunch

Palapa restaurants on the sand serve fresh fish, tacos, and cold drinks. Perfect for a long, lazy lunch between swims and sun. Laid-back and family-friendly.
