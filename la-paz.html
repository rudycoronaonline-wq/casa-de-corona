What to do Destination

La Paz

The capital of Baja California Sur. Calm water, whale sharks, and the gateway to Isla Espíritu Santo.

Whale Sharks

La Paz is one of the best places in the world to swim with whale sharks (season typically Oct–April). Guided tours take you by boat to where they feed; you snorkel alongside them in small groups. Responsible operators keep distance and limit impact.

Isla Espíritu Santo

A UNESCO World Heritage–listed island with turquoise coves, sea lions, and kayaking. Day trips from La Paz include snorkeling, lunch on the beach, and sometimes camping. Book through a local tour operator.

Malecón

The waterfront promenade is the heart of La Paz. Walk, bike, or sit with a drink and watch the sunset. Restaurants, cafés, and shops line the way. Quieter and more local than Cabo.
