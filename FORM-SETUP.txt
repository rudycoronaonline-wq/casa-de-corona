How to connect your booking form to Formspree
=============================================

Step 1 — Sign up
   • Open https://formspree.io in your browser.
   • Click "Get started" and create a free account (email or Google).

Step 2 — Create a form
   • After logging in, click "New form".
   • Give it a name (e.g. "Casa de Corona Booking").
   • Click "Create form".

Step 3 — Copy your form address
   • On the form page you'll see something like:
     "Your form endpoint: https://formspree.io/f/xyzabc12"
   • Copy the full URL (or just the part after /f/ — e.g. xyzabc12).

Step 4 — Paste it into your website
   • Open the file: casa-de-corona/main.js
   • Find this line (near the top of the booking section):
     var BOOKING_FORM_ENDPOINT = 'https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID';
   • Replace YOUR_FORM_ID with your form ID.
   • Example: if Formspree gave you https://formspree.io/f/mzbqnpzw
     then change the line to:
     var BOOKING_FORM_ENDPOINT = 'https://formspree.io/f/mzbqnpzw';
   • Save the file.

Step 5 — Test it
   • Open your site, go to the Book Direct section, fill out the form, and submit.
   • You should see "Thank you!" on the page and get an email from Formspree with the submission.

Done. New booking requests will go to your Formspree dashboard and to the email you used to sign up (you can change this in Formspree settings).
